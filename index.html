Drumming to the beat of your heart...

THE RULES

  • Click on 'Start Rec.' to start recording your music. (scroll down)
  • Press 'a' on keyboard to play Tom1 sound.
  • Press 'b' to play Tom2 sound, and so on till 'f' to play Drum sound.
  • Play to your heart's content!
  • Click on Stop Rec. to stop recording once you are done.
  • Click on 'Play Rec.' to listen to your music, 'Play Loop' to play in loop and 'Stop Loop Play' to refresh the page.
